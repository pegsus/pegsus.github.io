The Gorilla

Question: Can you guess the latin for gorilla? HINT: it's very easy!

There are two species of Gorilla:

  • Eastern gorilla, Gorilla beringei: The eastern gorilla can be found in the DRC, Rwanda and Uganda. It is highly endangered due to illegal hunting for bushmeat and deforestation destroying thier habitat. 70% of the species has been lost in the last 20 years but with your help conservation efforts can make movements to ensure the 4000 left can reproduce to repopulate the area.
  • Western gorilla, Gorilla beringei beringi: This is likley to be te gorilla you see at the zoo!Found in central Africa from Angola to Gabon, these gorillas face the same threats of bushmeat hunting and habitat destruction as thier eastern counterparts. There is low fecundity in females, meaning that they reproduce at slow rates. Your donation will go toward fertility programs to ensure populations do not dwindle.

The Orangutan

Fun Fact: In Malay orang means 'person' and utan means 'forest'- forest person!

Orangutans are arboreal , which means they spend most of thier time up in the trees of the Indonesian and Malasian rainforests.

Orangutans today are living widly in Borneo and Sumatra. The illegal pet trade and habitat destruction are the biggest threats to Orangutans.

Checking that the products you use at home do not contain palm oil is an important step in orangutan conservation, as this is often the reason deforestation occurs in these regions.
