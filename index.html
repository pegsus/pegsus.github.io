About Us

Prime Mates is dedicated to informing monkey lovers around the world about the current status of certain endangered species of primates and the steps you can take to help them.


Please take a moment to read about our monkeys, and donate to the causes we think our helping each species the most.


After all, they are our closest relatives!

The Gorilla

There are two species of Gorilla:

  • eastern lowland gorilla, Gorilla beringei
  • mountain gorilla, Gorilla beringei beringi
Both are found in central africa.
